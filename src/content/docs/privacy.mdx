While using our Site, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Like many site operators, we collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Site ("Log Data"). We may use your Personal Information to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials, and other relevant information. You can opt out of receiving these communications at any time.

## Cookies

Cookies are small files with data that may include a unique anonymous identifier. These are sent to your browser from our Site and stored on your device. Like many websites, we use cookies to collect information. You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is being sent. However, disabling cookies may limit your use of certain features on our Site.

The security of your Personal Information is important to us. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.
